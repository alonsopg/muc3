DEV-MUC3-1062 (TRW)

BOGOTA, 22 JAN 90 (INRAVISION TELEVISION CADENA 2) -- [TEXT] ATTENTION: THE MEDELLIN POLICE ARE CONFIRMING AT THIS TIME WHETHER IT IS TRUE THAT ALVARO DIEGO MONTOYA ESCOBAR, WHO IS THE SON OF THE SECRETARY GENERAL OF THE PRESIDENCY, HAS BEEN RELEASED NEAR THE CAMPOS DE PAZ CEMETARY OF THE CAPITAL OF ANTIOQUIA. AS YOU WILL RECALL, MONTOYA WAS KIDNAPPED ON 20 DECEMBER BY THE EXTRADITABLES.

ATTENTION: NEW REPORTS ON THE RELEASE OF ALVARO DIEGO MONTOYA, THE SON OF THE PRESIDENCY'S GENERAL SECRETARY, REVEAL THAT HE WAS FREED TODAY AT 0200 IN BOGOTA, AND NOT AT NOON IN MEDELLIN.
