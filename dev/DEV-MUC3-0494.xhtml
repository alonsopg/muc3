BOGOTA, 18 SEP 89 (INRAVISION TELEVISION CADENA 1) -- [TEXT] THIS IS A LAST-MINUTE REPORT: TWO MORE EXPLOSIONS HAVE TAKEN PLACE JUST A FEW MINUTES AGO IN BOGOTA. ONE BOMB EXPLODED IN WESTERN BOGOTA, ON BOYACA AVENUE AND ELDORADO AVENUE, WHILE THE OTHER OCCURRED AT THE TELEPHONE COMPANY SUBSTATION IN THE NORMANDY NEIGHBORHOOD. A WATCHMAN FIRED SHOTS AT THE TIME OF THE ATTACK, AND IT IS BELIEVED THAT THE ATTACKERS, WHO ARE RIDING IN A GREEN MAZDA, ARE WOUNDED.
