LIMA, 3 JUN 89 (TELEVISION PERUANA) -- [COMMUNIQUE] [LIMA NATIONAL SECURITY COMMAND] [TEXT] OFFICIAL COMMUNIQUE NUMBER 040.

1. TODAY AT 1245 A BUS CARRYING 27 ARMY SOLDIERS WAS BOMBED BY TERRORISTS NEAR THE 12TH BLOCK OF JUNIN STREET.

2. AS A RESULT OF THE ATTACK 5 SOLDIERS WERE KILLED, WHILE 1 OFFICER AND 14 SOLDIERS WERE WOUNDED. THE OTHER SOLDIERS RECEIVED MINOR BRUISES.

3. THE LIMA NATIONAL SECURITY COMMAND HAS ORDERED THE APPROPRIATE INVESTIGATION.

[DATED] LIMA, 3 JUNE 1989

[SIGNED] PUBLIC RELATIONS OFFICE OF THE LIMA NATIONAL SECURITY COMMAND
