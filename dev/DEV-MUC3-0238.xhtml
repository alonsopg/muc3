SAN SALVADOR, 9 JUN 89 (ACAN-EFE) -- [TEXT] SALVADORAN PRESIDENCY MINISTER ANTONIO RODRIGUEZ PORTH WAS ASSASSINATED TODAY IN SAN SALVADOR DURING AN ATTACK IN WHICH TWO MEMBERS OF HIS ESCORT WERE ALSO KILLED, THE POLICE HAVE REPORTED.

THE ATTACK, WHICH TOOK PLACE AT 0815 LOCAL (1415 GMT), WAS PERPETRATED IN THE CAPITAL CITY BY ALLEGED URBAN GUERRILLA COMMANDOS WHO MACHINEGUNNED THE MINISTER'S RESIDENCE, ACCORDING TO POLICE SOURCES.

RODRIGUEZ PORTH, 65, WAS SWORN IN AS MINISTER OF THE PRESIDENCY ON 1 JUNE. HE WAS ONE OF THE MAIN LEADERS OF THE NATIONALIST REPUBLICAN ALLIANCE (ARENA) AND OF THE SALVADORAN BUSINESS SECTOR.
