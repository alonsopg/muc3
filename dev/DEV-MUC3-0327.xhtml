LIMA, 4 AUG 89 (TELEVISION PERUANA) -- [EXCERPT] A SEVERE BLOW HAS BEEN DEALT TO THE SHINING PATH TERRORIST ORGANIZATION IN LIMA. IN THEIR TIRELESS STRUGGLE AGAINST SUBVERSION, THE POLICE TODAY PRESENTED TO THE PRESS 29 SHINING PATH MEMBERS WHO WERE ARRESTED IN DIFFERENT OPERATIONS. THE TERRORISTS WERE PRESENTED AT THE DIRCOTE [ANTITERRORIST DIRECTORATE] FACILITIES. INTERIOR MINISTER AGUSTIN MANTILLA HAS SAID THAT WITH THESE ARRESTS THE POLICE HAVE DISMANTLED A MAJOR SHINING PATH NETWORK.
