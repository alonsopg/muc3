BOGOTA, 7 SEP 89 (DPA) -- [TEXT] THE GOVERNMENT DECIDED TO TEMPORARILY SUSPEND CLASSES AT THE STATE-OWNED NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF COLOMBIA, THE LARGEST UNIVERSITY IN THE COUNTRY, AFTER TWO DEVICES EXPLODED ON CAMPUS.

ACCORDING TO PRELIMINARY REPORTS, SEVERAL HOODED MEN OCCUPIED A SQUARE ON CAMPUS AND BEGAN SHOUTING SLOGANS AGAINST THE UNITED STATES.

THESE INDIVIDUALS APPARENTLY EXPLODED THE DEVICES THAT WOUNDED TWO STUDENTS.
