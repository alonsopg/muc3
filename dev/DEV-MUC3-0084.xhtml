LIMA, 3 FEB 89 (EFE) -- [TEXT] MAOIST SHINING PATH GUERRILLAS HAVE KILLED THE MAYOR OF THE SMALL TOWN OF HOSTIGAL, AS WELL AS TWO PEASANTS, THE LOCAL PRESS REPORTED TODAY.

HOSPITAL IS LOCATED SOME 20 KM FROM YAUYOS PROVINCE, LIMA DEPARTMENT.

THE SHINING PATH GUERRILLAS ARRIVED IN HOSTIGAL LAST MONDAY MORNING AND FORCED PARISH PRIEST LUIS FUENTES TO SOUND THE ALARM IN ORDER TO GATHER THE PEOPLE.

MAYOR ARMANSIO QUISPE AND THE TWO PEASANTS WERE BROUGHT BEFORE A "KANGAROO COURT" BY THE SHINING PATH, AND THEN KILLED BY A FIRING SQUAD.

ACCORDING TO THE LATEST CALCULATIONS MADE BY THE PERUVIAN CONGRESS PACIFICATION COMMITTEE, ABOUT 13,000 PEOPLE HAVE BEEN KILLED IN PERU SINCE 1980 AS A RESULT OF POLITICAL VIOLENCE.
