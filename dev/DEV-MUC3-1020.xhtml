GUATEMALA CITY, 21 DEC 89 (ACAN-EFE) -- [TEXT] OFFICIAL SOURCES HAVE CONFIRMED THAT JORGE ADOLFO VARGAS, SECOND SECRETARY AT THE NICARAGUAN EMBASSY IN GUATEMALA, WAS SHOT TO DEATH TODAY IN GUATEMALA CITY BY SEVERAL UNIDENTIFIED MEN.

A SPOKESMAN FOR THE MUNICIPAL FIRE FIGHTERS TOLD ACAN-EFE THAT THE NICARAGUAN DIPLOMAT WAS WALKING IN A RESIDENTIAL AREA IN SOUTHERN GUATEMALA WHEN UNIDENTIFIED MEN IN A VEHICLE WITH DARKENED WINDOWS FIRED AT HIM WITH 9-MM WEAPONS.

THE SOURCE POINTED OUT THAT VARGAS, 38, WAS SHOT SIX TIMES AND DIED ON THE SPOT.

THE NICARAGUAN EMBASSY ALSO CONFIRMED THE REPORT FOR ACAN-EFE BUT OFFERED NO DETAILS.

SO FAR THE AUTHORITIES ARE UNAWARE OF THE MURDERERS' IDENTITIES OR THE MOTIVE FOR VARGAS GONZALEZ'S MURDER.
