BRASILIA (BRAZIL), 18 NOV 89 (RADIO NACIONAL DA AMAZONIA NETWORK) -- [TEXT] IN A NOTE RELEASED BY THE FOREIGN MINISTRY, THE BRAZILIAN GOVERNMENT STATES ITS INDIGNATION OVER THE NEWS ABOUT THE MURDER OF SIX JESUIT PRIESTS IN EL SALVADOR.

THE BRAZILIAN GOVERNMENT'S MESSAGE APPEALS TO THE PARTIES INVOLVED IN THE SALVADORAN CONFLICT TO PROMPTLY RESUME NEGOTIATIONS FOLLOWING THE OAS SUGGESTIONS REGARDING THE SEARCH FOR PEACE.
