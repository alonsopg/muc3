CLANDESTINE, 19 OCT 89 -- [TEXT] RUBEN ZAMORA, LEADER OF THE POPULAR SOCIAL CHRISTIAN MOVEMENT AND THE DEMOCRATIC CONVERGENCE, WAS UNHARMED FOLLOWING A TERRORIST ATTACK.

ZAMORA SAID THE COUNTRY IS SUFFERING FROM A WAVE OF VIOLENCE LAUNCHED BY GROUPS THAT DO NOT WANT THE NEGOTIATIONS TO SUCCEED. HE ALSO SAID THERE ARE ULTRARIGHTIST GROUPS THAT DO NOT SEEK A NEGOTIATED SETTLEMENT BECAUSE THEY BELIEVE THEIR INTERESTS WILL BE HARMED, AND ADDED THAT THERE ARE GROUPS WITHIN THE ARMED FORCES AND ARENA [NATIONALIST REPUBLICAN ALLIANCE] THAT DO NOT WANT THE NEGOTIATIONS. HE SAID THESE GROUPS TEND TO REACT WITH INCREASED VIOLENCE.
