CALI, 20 FEB 90 (RADIO CADENA NACIONAL) -- [TEXT] THE CALI POLICE HAVE CONFIRMED THAT U.S. PRIEST FRANCISCO ANTONIO AMICO FERRARI, WHO HAD BEEN KIDNAPPED BY AN ELN [ARMY OF NATIONAL LIBERATION] CELL, WAS RELEASED IN NORTHERN CALI ON 19 FEBRUARY.

PRIEST AMICO FERRARI, WHO HAS BEEN WORKING IN THE ANDRES SANIM NEIGHBORHOOD IN NORTHEASTERN CALI FOR 3 YEARS, SHOWED UP AT HIS PARISH, THE NUESTRA SENORA DE LA ASUNCION CHURCH, AT THE END OF THE 1930 MASS LAST NIGHT. ALTHOUGH VISIBLY TIRED, AMICO FERRARI WAS HAPPY AT HAVING BEEN FREED. THE PRIEST SAID THAT HE WAS TREATED WELL AND THAT HE HAD BEEN KIDNAPPED BY AN ELN CELL IN PROTEST OF U.S. PRESIDENT GEORGE BUSH'S RECENT VISIT TO CARTAGENA.
