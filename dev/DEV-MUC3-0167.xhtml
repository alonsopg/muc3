MANAGUA (NICARAGUA), 10 APR 89 (RADIO SANDINO) -- [TEXT] A MILITARY PATRIOTIC SERVICE [SMP] YOUTH WAS KILLED AND A SECOND ONE INJURED DURING A HONDURAN ARMY ATTACK AT EL PASO NARANJO, SOMOTILLO, CHINANDEGA DEPARTMENT.
