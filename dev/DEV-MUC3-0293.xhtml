MOSCOW (USSR), 14 JUL 89 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] PERUVIAN COMMUNISTS HAVE FIRMLY CONDEMNED THE TERRORIST ACT CARRIED OUT BY THE PERUVIAN REACTION AGAINST SOVIET FISHERMEN. AS HAS BEEN REPORTED, THE TERRORISTS PLACED A CHARGE OF DYNAMITE UNDER A BUS IN WHICH THERE WERE SOVIET FISHERMEN FROM SEVERAL TRAWLERS.

IN THE ANNOUNCEMENT BY THE PERUVIAN COMMUNIST PARTY CENTRAL COMMITTEE, IT IS POINTED OUT THAT THIS CRIME PURSUES THE AIM OF UNDERMINING RELATIONS BETWEEN PERU AND THE SOCIALIST COUNTRIES.
