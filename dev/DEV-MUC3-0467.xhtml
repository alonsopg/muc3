BOGOTA, 12 SEP 89 (RADIO CADENA NACIONAL) -- [TEXT] CALI -- THE JUDGES AND COURT EMPLOYEES OF THE CALI JUDICIAL DISTRICT HAVE BEEN ON STRIKE SINCE YESTERDAY TO PROTEST THE KIDNAPPING OF ORLANDO CUELLER OSORIO, A COURT PROCESS SERVER OF THE 12TH MUNICIPAL PENAL COURT. FOUR HEAVILY ARMED INDIVIDUALS, WHO POSED AS SECRET AGENTS, KIDNAPPED MR CUELLAR OSORIO FROM HIS HOME IN THE FLORALIA NEIGHBORHOOD, IN EASTERN CALI.

TELEPHONE CALLS MADE BY TERRORISTS SAY THE OFFICIAL HAS BEEN KILLED. THE AUTHORITIES HAVE BEEN UNABLE TO DETERMINE HIS WHEREABOUTS.
