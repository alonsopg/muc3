BOGOTA, 18 AUG 89 (EFE) -- [TEXT] THE MEDICAL AUTHORITIES HAVE REPORTED THAT LUIS CARLOS CARLOS GALAN, SENATOR AND PRESIDENTIAL HOPEFUL, DIED TONIGHT 2 HOURS AFTER HAVING BEEN SHOT IN AN ATTACK.

GALAN, 46, WAS MACHINEGUNNED TOGETHER WITH FIVE OTHER PERSONS WHO WERE WOUNDED WHEN HE WAS ABOUT TO DELIVER A SPEECH TONIGHT IN SOACHA, NEAR BOGOTA.
