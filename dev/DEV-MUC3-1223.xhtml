BOGOTA, 13 APR 90 (AFP) -- [TEXT] A SLIGHT TREMOR MEASURING THREE ON THE INTERNATIONAL SCALE OF ONE TO 12 ROCKED SOUTHWEST COLOMBIA AT 1027 LOCAL (1527 GMT) WITHOUT CAUSING ANY DAMAGE OR VICTIMS, THE OCCIDENTE OBSERVATORY IN THE CITY OF CALI, 500 KM FROM BOGOTA, HAS REPORTED.

THE QUAKE WAS HARDLY FELT, BUT FIRST AID ORGANIZATIONS IN CALI REPORTED THAT THEY HAD RECEIVED TELEPHONE CALLS FROM PEOPLE WHO DID FEEL IT AND WHO VOICED THEIR CONCERNS ABOUT THE RISKS THAT COULD BE RUN IN CHURCHES, WHICH ARE FILLED WITH PARISHIONERS ATTENDING GOOD FRIDAY MASSES.

THE QUAKE WAS ALSO SLIGHTLY FELT IN TOWNS LIKE TULUA, BUGA, BUENAVVENTURA, PASTO, PEREIRA, AND POPAYAN, A CITY WHICH WAS DESTROYED BY AN EARTHQUAKE ON MAUNDY THURSDAY IN 1983 KILLING APPROXIMATELY 200 PEOPLE AND INJURING OVER 500.
