DEV-MUC3-0224 (ADS)

BUENOS AIRES, 30 MAY 89 (NA) -- [TEXT] THE CIVIL DEFENSE OFFICE HAS REPORTED THAT FOUR PEOPLE MAY HAVE BEEN KILLED AND SEVEN WOUNDED IN A FIERCE CLASH BETWEEN POLICEMEN AND CIVILIANS. THE CIVILIANS WERE LOOTING STORES IN SAN MIGUEL, BUENOS AIRES PROVINCE.

IN A REPORT TO NA, IT WAS SAID THAT FOUR PEOPLE DIED IN INCIDENTS THAT OCCURRED THIS AFTERNOON AT 3400 MITRE AVENUE.
