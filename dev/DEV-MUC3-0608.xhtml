MEDELLIN, 26 OCT 89 (DPA) -- [TEXT] TWO POLICEMEN GUARDING THE ECUADORAN CONSULATE IN MEDELLIN WERE KILLED TODAY BY UNIDENTIFIED GUNMEN, IT WAS OFFICIALLY REPORTED.

POLICE SPOKESMEN REPORTED THAT THE TWO POLICEMEN WERE GUARDING THE DIPLOMATIC MISSION WHEN THREE MEN WALKING NEARBY, IN DOWNTOWN MEDELLIN, BEGAN FIRING AT THEM. THE AUTHORITIES SAID THEY DID NOT KNOW WHO IS RESPONSIBLE OR THE REASONS FOR THESE KILLINGS.
