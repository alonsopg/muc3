CLANDESTINE, 15 MAR 89 (RADIO VENCEREMOS) -- [TEXT] REPORTS FROM SAN SALVADOR INDICATE THAT AT 1910 TONIGHT, 15 MARCH, FIGHTING AND EXPLOSIONS CONTINUED IN VARIOUS NEIGHBORHOODS AND SECTIONS OF THE CITY. FIGHTING IS ESPECIALLY INTENSE IN THE SAN JACINTO NEIGHBORHOOD ON LOS DIPLOMATICOS AVENUE, WHERE A TRAFFIC JAM HAS RESULTED.

STRONG EXPLOSIONS HAVE ALSO BEEN HEARD IN THE 15 DE SEPTIEMBRE NEIGHBORHOOD, AND OUR REPORTS INDICATE THAT AT 1910, BARRICADES WERE STILL BURNING AND EXPLOSIONS COULD STILL BE HEARD ON EJERCITO BOULEVARD. BARRICADES AND EXPLOSIONS HAVE ALSO BEEN REPORTED NEAR BANCO CAPITALIZADOR, AND ATTACKS HAVE BEEN MADE AGAINST POLICE FORCES IN VARIOUS PARTS OF THE CAPITAL.
