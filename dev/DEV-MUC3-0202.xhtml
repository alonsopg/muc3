DEV-MUC3-0202 (ADS)

TEGUCIGALPA, 9 MAY 89 (VOZ DE HONDURAS NETWORK) -- [TEXT] WE HAVE LEARNED THAT THERE IS TALK OF A COUP IN GUATEMALA. SO FAR IT IS NOT KNOWN WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR THIS BLOW AGAINST DEMOCRACY IN GUATEMALA. PEOPLE WHO HAVE SPOKEN OVER THE TELEPHONE FROM GUATEMALA CITY WITH VOZ DE HONDURAS HAVE TOLD US THAT THERE ARE REPORTS IN GUATEMALA ABOUT A COUP D'ETAT THAT REPORTEDLY OCCURRED EARLY THIS MORNING. GUATEMALAN AIR FORCE PLANES ARE FLYING OVER THE GUATEMALAN CAPITAL. TO DATE THERE ARE NO EXACT REPORTS ON THE SITUATION. IT HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED THAT GUATEMALAN AIR FORCE PLANES ARE FLYING OVER VARIOUS PARTS OF GUATEMALA CITY.
