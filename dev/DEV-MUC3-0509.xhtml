BOGOTA, 22 SEP 89 (EMISORAS CARACOL NETWORK) -- [TEXT] THERE WAS AN EXPLOSION IN SOUTHERN BOGOTA TONIGHT AT ONE OF THE BOGOTA TELEPHONE COMPANY INSTALLATIONS. ACCORDING TO REPORTS JUST RECEIVED FROM THE METROPOLITAN POLICE, THE BUILDING WAS PARTIALLY DESTROYED.

A SECURITY GUARD, WHO WAS INSIDE THE BUILDING, WAS SERIOUSLY INJURED, THE OFFICIAL REPORT FROM THE BOGOTA POLICE SAID.

THE BOMB EXPLOSION TOOK PLACE IN OLAYA NEIGHBORHOOD ON THE CORNER OF 14TH AVENUE AND SOUTH 22D STREET IN SOUTHERN BOGOTA.
