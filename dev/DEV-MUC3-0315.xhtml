DEV-MUC3-0315 (INTEL TEXT PROC)

SAN SALVADOR, 29 JUL 89 (CANAL DOCE TELEVISION) -- [TEXT] THE FARABUNDO MARTI NATIONAL LIBERATION MARTI FRONT [FMLN] TODAY CLAIMED IT DOWNED TWO HELICOPTERS AND ALSO CHARGED SOME HOUSES WERE DESTROYED BY MILITARY AIRCRAFT IN NORTHERN MORAZAN DEPARTMENT. RADIO VENCEREMOS REPORTED MILITARY PLANES AND HELICOPTER GUNSHIPS BOMBED TOWNS AND VILLAGES NEAR THE CITY OF PERQUIN. ALTHOUGH THERE WERE NO VICTIMS, VENCEREMOS ADDED, THE BUILDING OF THE FOUNDATION FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF MORAZAN COMMUNITIES AND HOUSES AROUND A LOCAL SCHOOL WERE DESTROYED.

THE ARMED FORCES PRESS COMITTEE [COPREFA], HOWEVER, DENIED THE FMLN REPORTS. COPREFA SAID IT DID NOT KNOW OF ANY MILITARY UNITS CARRYING OUT COUNTERINSURGENCY OPERATIONS IN THAT REGION. IN ADDITION, COPREFA REPORTED THE GUERRILLAS BURNED HALF A MANZANA OF COFFEE FIELDS AT THE TRANSITORA FARM, SAN VICENTE DEPARTMENT. (ENDALL)
