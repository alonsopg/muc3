BOGOTA, 30 JAN 90 (INRAVISION TELEVISION CADENA 1) -- [TEXT] AT THIS TIME, THERE IS HEAVY FIGHTING AND EVEN ARMY BOMBING OF FARC [REVOLUTIONARY ARMED FORCES OF COLOMBIA] CAMPS NEAR URIBE IN META DEPARTMENT. THE CLASHES ARE TAKING PLACE IN JARDIN DE LAS PENAS, IN MESETAS MUNICIPALITY, NEAR WHERE THE HEADQUARTERS OF THE FARC SECRETARIAT IS LOCATED.

THIS REACTION BY THE MILITARY IS IN ANSWER TO A FARC AMBUSH OF A MILITARY PATROL ON 28 JANUARY, RESULTING IN FIVE SOLDIERS DEAD AND SEVEN WOUNDED. IN ADDITION, THE ARMY'S INTENSIVE ACTIONS ARE ALSO DUE TO THE DESIRE TO PUT A STOP TO FARC ATTACKS AND KIDNAPPINGS OF POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC LEADERS IN THE AREA.
