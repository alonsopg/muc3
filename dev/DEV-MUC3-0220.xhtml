DEV-MUC3-0220 (ADS)

BUENOS AIRES, 30 MAY 89 (NA) -- [TEXT] AN UNDETERMINED NUMBER OF WOUNDED PEOPLE, MOSTLY SUFFERING FROM CUTS, AND BROKEN WINDOWS AT THE ENTRANCE OF THE VELOX BANK ARE THE INITIAL RESULT OF THE EXPLOSION OF A BOMB AT 1215 (1515 GMT) ON THE DOWNTOWN CENTER OF SARMIENTO AND SAN MARTIN STREETS.

ACCORDING TO THE FIRST RESULTS OF THE POLICE INVESTIGATION, THE EXPLOSIVE DEVICE HAD BEEN LEFT ON A BICYCLE THAT HAD BEEN LEFT NEAR THE BANK.
