BOGOTA, 5 SEP 89 (AF) -- [TEXT] THE DEFENSE MINISTRY HAS REPORTED THAT A GROUP OF UNIDENTIFIED INDIVIDUALS TODAY KILLED ANGELA DE GUERRERO, WIFE OF COLONEL CARLOS GUERRERO OF THE ADMINISTRATION AND LOGISTICS DEPARTMENT OF THE ARMED FORCES GENERAL COMMAND, IN NORTHERN BOGOTA.

ACCORDING TO A MILITARY REPORT, ANGELA DE GUERRERO WAS WAITING FOR A RELATIVE IN FRONT OF A SUPERMARKET WHEN SHE WAS SHOT FROM A MOVING VEHICLE.

A POLICE OPERATION IS NOW BEING CARRIED OUT IN SEARCH OF THE CRIMINALS.
