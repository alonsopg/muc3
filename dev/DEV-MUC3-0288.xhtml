DEV-MUC3-0288 (INTEL TEXT PROC)

BOGOTA, 13 JUL 89 (EMISORAS CARACOL NETWORK) -- [TEXT] A NEW ATTACK AGAINST THE CANO - LIMON - COVENA OIL PIPELINE, WHICH CAUSED A LARGE OIL SPILL, WAS CARRIED OUT THIS MORNING BY TERRORISTS IN ARAUCA INTENDANCY. ACCORDING TO PRELIMINARY REPORTS, THE ATTACK OCCURRED NEAR THE KM - 45 MARKER IN ARAUCITA JURISDICTION, A SHORT DISTANCE FROM THE CANO - LIMON - COVENA OIL FIELDS, WHERE LARGE ARMY UNITS ARE BASED.
