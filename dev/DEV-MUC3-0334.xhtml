SAN SALVADOR, 8 AUG 89 (RADIO CADENA YSKL) -- [TEXT] A U.S. CITIZEN DIED AT THE DIAGNOSIS HOSPITAL EARLY THIS MORNING.

ACCORDING TO REPORTS WE RECEIVED, THE U.S. CITIZEN WAS SHOT LAST NIGHT WHEN UNIDENTIFIED INDIVIDUALS TRIED TO ENTER HIS RESIDENCE AT SAN SALVADOR'S VENEZUELA NEIGHBORHOOD, NEAR LAS COROLAS AVENUE. HE RECEIVED TWO BULLET WOUNDS AND WAS TAKEN TO THE DIAGNOSIS HOSPITAL, WHERE HE DIED EARLY THIS MORNING.

THE U.S. CITIZEN WAS IDENTIFIED AS BILLY MURPHY. HIS OCCUPATION IS STILL UNKNOWN.
