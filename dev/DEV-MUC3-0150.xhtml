LA PAZ, 21 MAR 89 (EFE) -- [TEXT] A BOMB PLANTED NEAR THE U.S. EMBASSY IN THIS CAPITAL WAS DEFUSED TODAY BY THE POLICE.

ACCORDING TO EARLY REPORTS FROM THE BOMB SQUAD, WHICH IS SUBORDINATE TO THE FIRE DEPARTMENT, AN ANONYMOUS CALL RECEIVED AT THE U.S. EMBASSY REPORTED THAT THE BOMB WAS ABOUT TO EXPLODE.

THE AMERICAN EMBASSY IN LA PAZ IS 3 BLOCKS FROM THE PLAZA DE ARMAS AND 3 BLOCKS FROM THE GOVERNMENT PALACE, AND THE LEGISLATIVE PALACE.

A TEAM FROM THE BOLIVIAN BOMB SQUAD MANAGED TO LOCATE AND DEFUSE THE BOMB AT 1810 (2210 GMT).

NO PERSON OR TERRORIST ORGANIZATION HAS YET CLAIMED RESPONSIBILITY FOR PLANTING THE BOMB.
