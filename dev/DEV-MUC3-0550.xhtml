BOGOTA, 6 OCT 89 (RADIO CADENA NACIONAL) -- [TEXT] TWO TERRORIST BOMBINGS IN NORTHERN BOGOTA HAVE WOUNDED SIX PERSONS AND CAUSED CONSIDERABLE DAMAGE.

THE COMMANDER OF THE METROPOLITAN POLICE REPORTED THAT THE FIRST BOMB WAS PLACED AT A COLPATRIA BANK BRANCH ON 86TH STREET AND 15TH AVENUE. THIS BOMB CAUSED HEAVY DAMAGE TO THE BANK AND WOUNDED SIX PASSERSBY. THE WOUNDED WERE TAKEN TO THE COUNTRY CLUB CLINIC, WHERE THEY WERE TREATED AND RELEASED.

THE SECOND BOMB WAS PLACED BY A YOUNG MAN AT THE TELEPHONE COMPANY OFFICE ON 91ST STREET AND 15TH AVENUE, CAUSING CONSIDERABLE DAMAGE. THE YOUNG MAN THEN ENTERED A NEARBY CARULLA SUPERMARKET, WHERE CITIZENS CAPTURED HIM.
