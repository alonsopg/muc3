BOGOTA, 22 SEP 89 (INRAVISION TELEVISION CADENA 2) -- [REPORT] [ALVARO AYALA] [EXCERPT] IN BOGOTA, ORDERS HAVE BEEN ISSUED TO PLACE BANKS UNDER PERMAMENT MILITARY PROTECTION. LAST NIGHT TWO BOMBS EXPLODED AT BRANCHES OF THE POPULAR AND OCCIDENTE BANKS IN DOWNTOWN BOGOTA. A MINOR WAS WOUNDED.

TO STOP THE WAVE OF ATTACKS ON BANKS AND FINANCIAL COMPANIES, THE DEFENSE MINISTRY THIS MORNING PLACED ALL BANKING AREAS IN THE CITY UNDER MILITARY CONTROL. THE SOLDIERS WILL REMAIN IN CHARGE [AL FRENTE] OF THESE INSTALLATIONS AROUND THE CLOCK.
