CLANDESTINE, 14 NOV 89 (RADIO VENCEREMOS) -- [TEXT] ATTENTION, THIS IS A LAST-MINUTE REPORT FROM SAN SALVADOR: OUR FORCES HAVE OCCUPIED POSITIONS HELD BY THE NATIONAL POLICE IN CUSCATANCINGO. THE POLICEMEN WERE FORCED TO FLEE IN DISARRARY FROM A POSITION THAT THEY HAD HELD IN CUSCATANCINGO, NORTHERN SAN SALVADOR.

MEANWHILE, THE ENEMY IS USING M-79 ROCKET LAUNCHERS IN DOWNTOWN MEJICANOS. THE MARKET AREA AND THE PROJECTS HAVE BEEN AFFECTED.

WE REPEAT: A NATIONAL POLICE POSITION WAS ATTACKED BETWEEN 1310 AND 1350 [1910 AND 1950 GMT]. THE POLICEMEN, WHO ARE EXCELLENT AT REPRESSING THE PEOPLE AND THE COMMITTEES OF MOTHERS, FLED CUSCATANCINGO IN DISARRAY AFTER BEING ATTACKED BY FARABUNDO MARTI NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT FORCES.

AT 1407 [2007 GMT], AN A-37 DRAGONFLY PLANE OVERFLEW AYUTUXTEPEQUE, MEJICANOS, AND ZACAMIL. OUR FIGHTERS ARE TRAINED TO FIRE AT THESE PLANES. THIRTY MINUTES AGO, A PUSH-AND-PULL 0-2 PLANE DROPPED BOMBS ON ZACAMIL NEIGHBORHOOD AND A C-47 MACHINEGUNNED THE AREA.

AN A-37 PLANE IS NOW OVERFLYING NORTHERN SAN SALVADOR AFTER OUR COMRADES OCCUPIED A POSITION THAT THE NATIONAL POLICE HAD HELD IN CUSCATANCINGO.
