BUENOS AIRES, 10 JAN 90 (NA) -- [TEXT] A UCR [RADICAL CIVIC UNION] BUENOS AIRES PROVINCE COMMITTEE SPOKESMAN TODAY REPORTED THAT A UCR DISTRICT HEADQUARTERS IN BUENOS AIRES PROVINCE WAS "COMPLETELY DESTROYED BY A BOMB EXPLOSION." THE DESTROYED UCR HEADQUARTERS IS IN THE MORENO DISTRICT OF BUENOS AIRES.

THE SPOKESMAN ADDED THAT NO ONE WAS INJURED AND THAT LEOPOLDO MOREAU, THE UCR BUENOS AIRES PROVINCE LEADER, HAS TRAVELLED TO MORENO.

IT IS EXPECTED THAT UCR BUENOS AIRES PROVINCE BOARD WILL MEET AND THAT THEY WILL ASK THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT AND THE INTERIOR MINISTRY TO INVESTIGATE THE INCIDENT.
