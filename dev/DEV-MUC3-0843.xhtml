SAN SALVADOR, 29 NOV 89 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] NATIONAL GUARD PERSONNEL STATIONED IN SAN MIGUEL HAVE CAPTURED THE FMLN [FARABUNDO MARTI NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT] TERRORIST COMMANDER WHO GOES BY THE PSEUDONYM ARISTIDES; THIS TOOK PLACE IN A NEIGHBORHOOD IN THAT TOWN. ARISTIDES HAD BEEN OPERATING IN A LARGE SECTOR OF THE EASTERN PART OF THE COUNTRY FOR SOME TIME, AND HAD COMMITTED ALL TYPES OF CRIMES AGAINST THE SALVADORAN PEOPLE.

COMMANDER ARISTIDES HAS PROVIDED THE ARMED FORCES WITH IMPORTANT INFORMATION DIRECTLY LINKING THE CYNICAL NICARAGUAN PRESIDENT DANIEL ORTEGA'S GOVERNMENT TO THE FMLN.
