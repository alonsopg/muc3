DEV-MUC3-1032 (TRW)

SANTIAGO, 28 DEC 89 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] A BOMB EXPLODED IN THE BATHROOM OF THE CHILEAN-U.S. CULTURAL INSTITUTE AT 1467 MONEDA STREET WHILE 360 STUDENTS WERE IN THE BUILDING. THE POLICE HAVE SAID THAT THE BOMB CONTAINED 1 KG OF EXPLOSIVES MADE UP OF AMMONIUM GELIGNITE AND ANFO [AMMONIUM NITRATE- FUEL OIL]. THE BOMB WAS SET OFF BY A FUSE SYSTEM.

THE BOMB CAUSED SERIOUS DAMAGE TO THE 5TH-FLOOR MENS ROOM. THE POLICE REPORTED THAT THE EXPLOSION CAUSED A PANIC BUT NO CASUALTIES.
