BOGOTA, 13 FEB 90 (RADIO CADENA NACIONAL) -- [REPORT] [CARLOS PERDOMO] [TEXT] ONE OF THE U.S. CITIZENS WHO WAS KIDNAPPED IN MEDELLIN TODAY, PRESUMABLY BY GUERRILLAS OF THE ARMY OF NATIONAL LIBERATION [ELN], WAS RELEASED A FEW HOURS AFTER BEING TAKEN HOSTAGE. ACCORDING TO A POLICE REPORT, THE GUERRILLAS TOLD THE VICTIM THAT HIS KIDNAPPING WAS A WAY FOR HIS CAPTORS TO PROTEST PRESIDENT GEORGE BUSH'S VISIT TO COLOMBIA.
