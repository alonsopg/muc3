BOGOTA, 29 APR 90 (EFE) -- [TEXT] ANTONIO NAVARRO WOLFF, WHO HAD BEEN THE NUMBER TWO MAN IN THE 19 APRIL MOVEMENT (M-19), WAS NOMINATED TODAY AS THE NEW PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE TO REPLACE CARLOS PIZARRO LEONGOMEZ, WHO WAS MURDERED ON 26 APRIL.

NAVARRO WOLFF, WHO IS A FORMER GUERRILLA LIKE THE SLAIN LEADER, RECEIVED THE NOMINATION WHEN PIZARRO'S FUNERAL PROCESSION STOPPED AT THE HOME OF LIBERATOR SIMON BOLIVAR IN BOGOTA.

IN A SPEECH MADE TO THE THOUSANDS AT THE FUNERAL, NAVARRO ACCEPTED THE NOMINATION AND SAID THAT HE WAS PICKING UP THE BANNERS OF THE SLAIN LEFTIST LEADER.
