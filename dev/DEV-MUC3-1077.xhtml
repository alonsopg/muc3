GARCIA-PENA ARCHILA WAS RECENTLY APPOINTED ADVISER TO THE COLOMBIAN EMBASSY IN KOREA, A POST HE WAS SCHEDULED TO ASSUME FRIDAY, THE DAY HE DISAPPEARED.
