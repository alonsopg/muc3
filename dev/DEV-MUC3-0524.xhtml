BOGOTA, 26 SEP 89 (DPA) -- [TEXT] IN A NEW ACTION ATTRIBUTED TO THE DRUG TRAFFICKING RINGS OPERATING IN BOGOTA, A MEDIUM-SIZED BOMB PARTIALLY DESTROYED A SECONDARY SCHOOL LOCATED IN A POPULOUS SOUTHERN BOGOTA NEIGHBORHOOD TONIGHT.

IT WAS REPORTED THAT THE EXPLOSION SEVERELY DAMAGED THE SAN IGNACIO DE LOYOLA SCHOOL, LOCATED A SHORT DISTANCE FROM A POLICE STATION.

AUTHORITIES SAID THE EXPLOSION CAUSED NO CASUALTIES, ALTHOUGH IT RESULTED IN LOSSES AMOUNTING TO MILLIONS.

THE DYNAMITE ATTACK, THE 41ST IN BOGOTA DURING THIS MONTH, OCCURRED ONLY A FEW HOURS AFTER ANOTHER EXPLOSION DESTROYED ONE STORE OF A SUPERMARKET CHAIN.

NUMEROUS THREATS SAYING THAT BOMBS HAD BEEN PLANTED IN SCHOOLS HAVE BEEN REPORTED IN THE "WAR" THAT THE DRUG CARTELS HAVE DECLARED IN REPRISAL FOR THE GOVERNMENT'S INTENSE OFFENSIVE AGAINST THEM.
