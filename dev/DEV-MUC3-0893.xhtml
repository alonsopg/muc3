SAN SALVADOR, 28 NOV 89 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] THERE IS PROFOUND INDIGNATION OVER THE MURDER OF DR. FRANCISCO JOSE GUERRERO, PRESIDENT OF THE SUPREME COURT OF JUSTICE. THE MURDER WAS COMMITTED BY TERRORISTS AT ABOUT 0930 [1530 GMT] THIS MORNING. ACCORDING TO WITNESSES, THE TERRORISTS DROVE A YELLOW VOLKSWAGEN.

PRESIDENT ALFREDO CRISTIANI ORDERED A COMPLETE INVESTIGATION TO DETERMINE WHO MURDERED DR GUERRERO AND TO BRING THEM TO TRIAL ACCORDING TO THE SALVADORAN PENAL CODE, WHICH WAS MODIFIED A FEW DAYS AGO. BOTH PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTORS HAVE SPOKEN OUT AGAINST THESE CRIMES, BECAUSE THEY ONLY BRING MORE GRIEF AND PAIN TO THE SUFFERING SALVADORAN PEOPLE.
