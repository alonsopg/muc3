MEDELLIN, 17 JAN 90 (AFP) -- [TEXT] THE EXTRADITABLES, A COLOMBIAN MAFIA GROUP LINKED WITH DRUG TRAFFICKING ACTIVITIES, ANNOUNCED TODAY THAT THEY WILL LAY DOWN THEIR WEAPONS AND SUSPEND THEIR COCAINE SHIPMENTS. THE EXTRADITABLES ADMITTED TO THE GOVERNMENT'S VICTORY AND ADMITTED THAT THEY WANT PEACE IN COLOMBIA.

THE EXTRADITABLES ALSO ANNOUNCED THAT THEY WILL RELEASE ALL THE PEOPLE THEY HAVE ABDUCTED. THEY ADDED THAT THERE WILL NOT BE ANY MORE BOMBINGS, TERRORIST ATTACKS OR MURDERS OF GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS, LAWYERS, AND JOURNALISTS.

THE EXTRADITABLES' POSITION IS STATED IN A COMMUNIQUE SENT TO PRESIDENT VIRGILIO BARCO'S GOVERNMENT BY PATRICIA AND DINA ECHAVARRIA, TWO WOMEN WHO HAVE BEEN HELD BY THE EXTRADITABLES SINCE 16 DECEMBER IN MEDELLIN, THE CAPITAL CITY OF NORTHWESTERN ANTIOQUIA DEPARTMENT.
