SANTIAGO, 18 AUG 89 (LA TERCERA DE LA HORA) -- [EXCERPTS] ARICA -- TWO BOLIVIANS CARRYING 2.5 KG PURE COCAINE IN A HANDBAG HAVE BEEN ARRESTED BY CARABINEROS OS-7 DEPARTMENT PERSONNEL. THE DRUG TRAFFICKERS ENTERED THE COUNTRY ON BYCICLES THROUGH THE AREA OF VISVIRI.

THE DETAINEES ARE JORGE GONZALEZ LLUCRA, 20, AND MARIO GONZALEZ LLUCRA, 19. [PASSAGE OMITTED]

THE POLICE ALSOS SEIZED A BROWING PISTOL FROM ONE OF THE DRUG TRAFFICKERS, WHO FIRED THREE SHOTS BUT HIT NO ONE. [PASSAGE OMITTED]
