CLANDESTINE, 18 NOV 89 (RADIO VENCEREMOS) -- [TEXT] WE URGE THE U.S. GOVERNMENT TO IMMEDIATELY WITHDRAW FROM THE ILOPANGO AIR BASE THE U.S. PILOTS WHO ARE HERE TO PARTICIPATE IN AIR BOMBINGS DURING THE NIGHT TO RELIEVE THE TIRED AND GENOCIDAL PILOTS OF GENERAL JUAN RAFAEL BUSTILLO.

WE URGE THE U.S. EMBASSY TO WITHDRAW THE U.S. PILOTS. OTHERWISE, THE NEXT PLANES AND HELICOPTERS WILL BE DOWNED WITH U.S. PILOTS -- LIKE THOSE WHO HAVE COME FROM A U.S. BASE IN HONDURAS -- ON BOARD.
