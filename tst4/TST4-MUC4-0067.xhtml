SANTIAGO, 7 NOV 88 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] CARABINEROS HAVE FOUND FOUR PACKETS CONTAINING EXPLOSIVES AT THE FOOT OF HIGH TENSION ELECTRIC TOWER NO 657 OF THE COLBUN-MACHICURA LINE.

THE PACKETS WERE FOUND IN PLOT NO 5, EL ROMERAL SECTOR, 2 KM WEST OF SOUTHERN HIGHWAY NO 5.

THE LAND BELONGS TO RAMON URRUTIA BLANCO.

EACH PACKET CONTAINED 2 KG OF AMONIUM GELIGNITE, DETONATORS, FUSES, AND TIMING DEVICES.

SPECIALIZED PERSONNEL BELIEVE THAT THE BOMBS WERE PLANTED A FEW DAYS AGO SO THAT THEY COULD BE ACTIVATED AT ANY TIME.
