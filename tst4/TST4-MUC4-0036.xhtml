LIMA, 12 OCT 88 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] THE POLITICAL-MILITARY COMMAND HAS ISSUED A COMMUNIQUE REPORTING THAT THREE TERRORISTS HAVE DIED AND FOUR WERE ARRESTED IN AN ARMED CLASH BETWEEN AN ARMY PATROL AND A TERRORIST COLUMN.

THE COMMUNIQUE SAYS THAT A FEW DAYS AGO A GROUP OF TERRORISTS CLASHED WITH THE ARMY IN THE CHILHUA REGION, LA MAR PROVINCE, AYACUCHO DEPARTMENT. SOME TERRORISTS WERE DETAINED AND PUT AT THE DISPOSAL OF THE SAN MIGUEL POLICE STATION.
