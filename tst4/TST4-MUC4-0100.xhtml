LIMA, 27 DEC 88 (EFE) -- [EXCERPT] THE PERUVIAN POLICE TODAY ANNOUNCED THAT IT WILL STRENGTHEN SECURITY ON HIGH-TENSION TOWERS, ONE OF THE MAIN TERRORIST TARGETS IN PERU, ON NEW YEAR'S EVE.

CIVIL GUARDS DIRECTOR LIEUTENANT GENERAL RUBEN ROMERO SAID THAT THE NUMBER OF POLICE TROOPS GUARDING THE TOWERS WILL BE INCREASED TO PREVENT ATTACKS THAT COULD CAUSE BLACKOUTS IN LIMA AND OTHER AREAS. [PASSAGE OMITTED]
