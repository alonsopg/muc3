SAN SALVADOR, 1 NOV 88 (CANAL DOCE TELEVISION) -- [TEXT] THE ARMED FORCES HAVE REPORTED THAT TWO SOLDIERS AND ONE GUERRILLA WERE KILLED YESTERDAY IN FIGHTING IN SAN MIGUEL AND MORAZAN DEPARTMENTS. THEY ALSO REPORTED THAT THE ANTEL [NATIONAL ADMINISTRATION FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS] OFFICE, COURT OFFICES, AND COMMUNITY CENTER OF DULCE NOMBRE DE MARIA WERE DYNAMITED BY THE FMLN EARLY THIS MORNING.

ACCORDING TO THE REPORTS, A LARGE GROUP OF REBELS ENTERED THE TOWN AND, AFTER SUBDUING THE MILITARY GUARDS, THEY DYNAMITED THE BUILDINGS. NO CASUALTIES WERE REPORTED.
