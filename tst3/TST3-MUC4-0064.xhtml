SAN SALVADOR, 18 NOV 89 (DOMESTIC SERVICE) -- [TEXT] THE SALVADORAN ARMED FORCES AND SECURITY CORPS HAVE INFLICTED THE FOLLOWING CASUALTIES ON THE ENEMY: 784 DEAD, 357 WOUNDED. THE ARMED FORCES ALSO SEIZED THE FOLLOWING EQUIPMENT: 376 RIFLES, 3 CARBINES, 11 SHOTGUNS, 83 ROCKET LAUNCHERS, 26 MORTARS, 8 MACHINEGUNS, 17 SUBMACHINEGUNS, 31 PISTOLS, 17 RADIOS.

THIS HAS BEEN A REPORT ON THE CASUALTIES INFLICTED BY OUR GLORIOUS ARMED FORCES ON THE FMLN [FARABUNDO MARTI NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT] CRIMINAL TERRORISTS. THE REPORT INCLUDED THE WAR MATERIEL SEIZED IN THE LAST FEW HOURS FROM THE FMLN COMMUNISTS.
