BUENOS AIRES, 7 FEB 89 (DYN) -- [TEXT] POLICE SOURCES HAVE REPORTED THAT AT LEAST FOUR UNIDENTIFIED INDIVIDUALS IN A CAR TODAY SHOT AT A SENTRY POST OF THE LA TABLADA 3D INFANTRY REGIMENT AND SWIFTLY FLED.

THE ATTACK TOOK PLACE AT 0340 TODAY. A CAR WAS TRAVELING VERY FAST ALONG CONSTITUYENTES AVENUE. SHOTS WERE FIRED FROM IT AT THE SENTRY POST FROM A DISTANCE OF SOME 150 METERS.

ACCORDING TO THE SOURCES THE ATTACKERS THEN FLED. THEY HAVE NOT YET BEEN IDENTIFIED.
