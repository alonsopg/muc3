LIMA, 1 FEB 89 (EFE) -- [EXCERPT] IN A COMMUNIQUE ADDRESSED TO THE LOCAL MEDIA, THE PERUVIAN GUERRILLA MOVEMENT TUPAC AMARU REVOLUTIONARY MOVEMENT (MRTA) TODAY CLAIMED RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE KIDNAPPING OF 102 PEOPLE.

AMONG THE MANY BUSINESSMEN WHO HAVE BEEN KIDNAPED FROM DIFFERENT INDUSTRIAL AND BUSINESS SECTORS IS CARLOS ENRIQUE FERREYROS, ALIAS "THE KIND OF THE MACHINERY INDUSTRY." FERREYROS WAS KIDNAPED ON 2 DECEMBER. THE MRTA IS ASKING $10 MILLION RANSOM FOR FERREYROS' RELEASE. THIS IS THE LARGEST RANSOM THE MRTA HAS EVER REQUESTED. [PASSAGE OMITTED]
