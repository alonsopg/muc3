SAN SALVADOR, 30 NOV 89 (RADIO CADENA CUSCATLAN) -- [COMMUNIQUE] [ARMED FORCES] [TEXT] THROUGH THESE MEANS, THE ARMED FORCES HEREBY NOTIFIES THE SALVADORAN PEOPLE, SPECIFICALLY THOSE LIVING IN THE ESCALON RESIDENTIAL AREA OF SAN SALVADOR, THAT THEY SHOULD NOT HEED RUMORS CIRCULATING TO THE EFFECT THAT THE ESCALON RESIDENTIAL AREA WILL BE BOMBED. THIS IS FALSE. ANY ACTIONS AGAINST INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM WILL BE ANNOUNCED IN DUE TIME. THE RESIDENTS OF ESCALON SHOULD REST ASSURED AND NOT LEAVE THEIR HOMES.
