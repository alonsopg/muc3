CARACAS (VENEZUELA), 15 JUL 89 (DPA) -- [TEXT] VENEZUELAN PRESIDENT CARLOS ANDRES PEREZ' BROTHER-IN-LAW WAS KIDNAPPED IN COLOMBIA, ACCORDING TO FAMILY SOURCES IN CARACAS. A REPORT BROADCAST ON A PRIVATE TELEVISION CHANNEL STATED THAT UNIDENTIFIED ASSAILANTS KIDNAPPED NORBERTO RODRIGUEZ -- BROTHER OF VENEZUELA'S FIRST LADY BLANCA RODRIGUEZ DE PEREZ -- IN COLOMBIA'S NORTHERN SANTANDER DEPARTMENT, WHERE HE WAS TENDING TO PERSONAL MATTERS.

ACCORDING TO THE TELEVISION REPORT, THE VENEZUELAN GOVERNMENT CONTACTED COLOMBIAN AUTHORITIES TO OBTAIN MORE INFORMATION ON THE INCIDENT. NO REASONS WERE GIVEN AS TO WHY RODRIGUEZ' RELATIVES FEAR THAT HE MIGHT HAVE BEEN KIDNAPPED.
