GUATEMALA CITY, 22 DEC 89 (EL GRAFICO) -- [TEXT] THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF GUATEMALA YESTERDAY STRONGLY CONDEMNED THE MURDER OF JORGE ADOLFO VARGAS GONZALEZ, SECOND SECRETARY OF THE NICARAGUAN EMBASSY IN GUATEMALA. VARGAS WAS MURDERED YESTERDAY MORNING IN ZONE 10 OF THIS CAPITAL. CLAUDIA ARENAS, PUBLIC RELATIONS SECRETARY OF THE PRESIDENCY, HAS SAID THAT "THIS IS A REGRETTABLE ACT THAT WE STRONGLY CONDEMN."

SHE ADDED THAT "DURING A TALK WITH INTERIOR MINISTER CARLOS A. MORALES VILLATORO, IT WAS REPORTED THAT AN INVESTIGATION OF THE MURDER WAS IMMEDIATELY ORDERED TO UNCOVER THE MURDERERS, ARREST THEM, AND BRING THEM TO COURT."

THE GOVERNMENT SPOKESPERSON ADDED THAT "THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC DEEPLY REGRETS THAT THIS VIOLENT ACTION HAS TAKEN PLACE AGAINST A MEMBER OF THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS ACCREDITED TO OUR COUNTRY."

THE MURDERED DIPLOMAT, JORGE ADOLFO VARGAS GONZALEZ, WAS THE SECOND SECRETARY OF THE NICARAGUAN EMBASSY IN GUATEMALA.

FOREIGN MINISTER ARIEL RIVERA THIS MORNING ALSO CONDEMNED THE MURDER OF THE SECOND SECRETARY OF THE NICARAGUAN EMBASSY IN GUATEMALA, JORGE ADOLFO VARGAS GONZALEZ.
