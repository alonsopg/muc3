SAN SALVADOR, 8 MAR 90 (AFP) -- [TEXT] HEAVY FIGHTING BROKE OUT IN THE NORTHERN PART OF SAN SALVADOR TONIGHT AT ABOUT 2115 (0315 GMT), AND AS OF NOW IT IS STILL GOING ON.

SAN SALVADOR IS BEING SHAKEN BY STRONG DETONATIONS OF HEAVY WEAPONS AND INTENSE RIFLE AND MACHINEGUN FIRE, WHILE HELICOPTERS ARE FLYING OVER THE AREA WHERE THE FIGHTING IS TAKING PLACE.

THE FIGHTING SEEMS TO BE CENTERED IN THE SURROUNDING NEIGHBORHOODS OF AYUTUXTEPEQUE, COLONIA ESCANDIA, SAN RAMON, AND CUSCATANCINGO, WHERE THE REBELS REPORTEDLY BLEW UP SEVERAL ELECTRICITY POLES WITH DYNAMITE, CAUSING POWER OUTAGES IN VARIOUS SECTORS OF THE CAPITAL CITY.

A MILITARY SOURCE SAID THAT TROOPS FROM THE 1ST INFANTRY BRIGADE HAVE BEEN DISPATCHED TO THE AREAS WHERE THE CLASHES ARE TAKING PLACE.
