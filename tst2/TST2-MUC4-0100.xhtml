BRASILIA (BRAZIL), 26 MAY 89 (RADIO NACIONAL DA AMAZONIA NETWORK) -- [TEXT] THE BRAZILIAN EMBASSY IN COLOMBIA HAS CONFIRMED THE RELEASE OF REDE GLOBO JOURNALIST CARLOS MARCELO WHO WAS KIDNAPPED BY COLOMBIAN ARMY OF NATIONAL LIBERATION GUERRILLAS.

MARCELO WAS WRITING AN ARTICLE ON THE KIDNAPPING OF THE THREE BRASPETRO [PETROBRAS INTERNATIONAL, INC.] ENGINEERS WHEN HE WAS ABDUCTED BY THE GUERRILLAS.

THE GUERRILLAS SENT A COMMUNIQUE THROUGH THIS JOURNALIST SAYING THAT THEY WILL ONLY RELEASE THE ENGINEERS WHEN COLOMBIA DECIDES TO NATIONALIZE THE PETROLEUM SECTOR.
